Privacy Policy for NFL Live Hub

Last updated: November 5, 2023

Introduction

Our Chrome extension, NFL Live Hub, is committed to protecting the privacy of our users. This Privacy Policy outlines our practices concerning the collection, use, and sharing of user data.

Data Collection

The extension uses the YouTube API to display videos and the Arena Chat widget to provide chat functionality. When you use our extension, we may collect data related to your use of these features as follows:

  • Interaction data with YouTube videos
  • Messages and interactions within Arena Chat

Data Use

We use the data collected to:

  • Enhance the functionality of our extension
  • Provide personalized content and recommendations
  • Understand and analyze the usage trends and preferences of our users

Data Sharing

We share user data with the following parties:

  • YouTube, as governed by their privacy policy
  • Arena Chat, in accordance with their privacy practices

We do not sell or rent user data to marketers or third parties.

Third-Party Services

Our extension integrates with third-party services, namely YouTube and Arena Chat, and their use of your information is governed by their respective privacy policies.

Your Rights

You have the right to request access to the personal data we hold about you and to ask that your personal data be corrected, updated, or deleted. If you would like to exercise this right, please contact us through the contact information below.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time in order to reflect, for example, changes to our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons.

Contact Us
