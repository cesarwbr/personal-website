Privacy Policy for Legal AI Extension

Last Updated: December 3, 2024

Data Collection and Usage

Legal AI collects and processes:

  • Website privacy policy text for AI analysis
  • User preferences and settings
  • Analysis results cache

We do not collect:

  • Personal information
  • Browsing history
  • Authentication credentials
  • Payment information

Data Storage

  • All data is stored locally in your browser
  • No data is transmitted to external servers
  • Analysis results are cached locally to improve performance

Third-Party Services

  • The extension uses local AI processing only
  • No data is shared with third parties
  • No external analytics or tracking services are used

User Rights

You have the right to:

  • Clear cached data through browser settings
  • Disable or uninstall the extension at any time
  • Control when analysis is performed via manual activation

Security

  • All processing occurs locally within your browser
  • No data leaves your device
  • No network connections are established for analysis

Updates

  • Policy updates will be communicated through extension updates
  • Significant changes will be highlighted in release notes
  • Users will be notified of material changes

Contact

  • Email: cesarwbr@gmail.com
  • GitHub Issues: github.com/cesarwbr/legal-ai-extension/issues

Changes to This Policy

We reserve the right to update this privacy policy. Users will be notified of any material changes through the extension's update mechanism.
